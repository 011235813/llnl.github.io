[Flux](https://github.com/flux-framework/flux-core) is a flexible framework for resource management consisting of a suite of projects, tools, and libraries which may be used to build site-custom resource managers for HPC centers. This version includes enhancements to inject functionality, the queue state, `flux-jobs`, and more.
